About me

I am a City and Guilds qualified Piano Tuner/Technician with a diploma in tuning, maintenance and repairs from Newark Technical College.After gaining valuable experience with Winchester’s own music shop, Whitwam’s music in 1982 as a piano tuner servicing new pianos, as well as a large clientele in the Winchester and wider Hampshire area, I establsihed my own tuning and repair business in 1984.

Services Provided

Tuning

Utilizing the "traditional method” of tuning. This relies on pitching the notes by ear and gauging the playability by touch and feel.

Consultation

Advice is offered on all aspects of piano, including maintenance and care of your piano.

Repairs

From sticking keys to a full restring, most repairs can be undertaken in situ.
